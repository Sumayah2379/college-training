AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines or software that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and recognizing patterns. AI is used in everyday applications such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, self-driving cars, and more. It is rapidly transforming industries and reshaping the way we live and work.
